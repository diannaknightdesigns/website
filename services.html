Services

A well-designed space should offer comfort, practicality and visual appeal. Give Dianna a call to talk about your design needs. Interior design services are available in the GTA including Brampton, Oakville, Mississauga, and Toronto.

The scope of services is tailored to the needs of the client and the home itself. These services are available in three different packages, and can be customized to fit your needs.

Initial Consultation

After a short phone interview, a 2-hour personalized in-home visit is scheduled. This can be a stand along meeting or serve as the start of a design project, where I help you create a vision for the space and ideas for furniture placement, colours and accessories. This is a perfect start for a DIY home owner.

Due to Covid-19, a one hour Virtual Design walkthrough is also available with a follow up email summarizing options and recommendations and any sketches. It's fast and easy to equip you with tools to follow through on your wishes.

In-Home: $300 + HST & Travel
Virtual: $200 + HST.

Design Concept & Development

I look forward to working collaboratively with you to create a soothing or stimulating environment. From room layout to custom sourced furniture and fabric selections, art and accessories, I help make your dream room come true.

Measurements are taken and a 3D virtual room is created to simulate the space and selections so you can 'see and feel' what one room will look like. A spread sheet with pricing and furniture details with dimensions is included. All selections are confirmed by the client after a 1-2 hour in house presentation with fabric samples. When necessary, Dianna will meet the client for a sit test of furniture to confirm the best comfort and fit at a trade only location for best pricing and wide selection. At this point, clients can choose to proceed on their own. Typically 18-20 hours are required for a virtual room with suggested selections.

$1500 + HST.

Install & Completion

After clients have confirmed all selections, pricing and the furniture layout after the presentation, Dianna works to implement the plan including placing orders, tracking items and arranging deliveries. Any discounts are shared with the client which often offset the design costs. On reveal day, extra styling items are brought in to finish the space and show a fully styled room. These items complete the look and are optional for the client to purchase at cost.

$90/h + HST.
